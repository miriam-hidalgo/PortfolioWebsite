Miriam Hidalgo

Welcome to my portfolio page! My name is Miriam Hidalgo and I am an avid web developer. I studied Psychology and have heavy experience in social service and adminstrative work. Regardless of the setting, I often found myself troubleshooting tech issues and creating systems to automate many of the typical day to day problems presented to me. Little did I know I have been utilizing the same building blocks mirrored in programming in that a problem would arise and I would have to break it down in smaller steps to fix. I am happy to say that my eagerness to solve daily work challenges inspired me to complete a Coding Bootcamp which taught me to channel my ability to problem-solve into the practicum of coding. Thank you for taking the time to visit my page. Please feel free to browse and check out some of the projects that illustrate my coding journey!
